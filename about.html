About Mosquito Man Plus

A dedicated mosquito line from a team that already knows Durham Region.

Mosquito Man Plus was started by Joe Barrett after years of running Bugman Plus, the Oshawa pest control company built in response to the region's rising pest pressure.

Brand Story

One job, done thoroughly, instead of twenty jobs done adequately.

Bugman Plus already covered mosquitoes as one of twenty pest services. But mosquito calls behaved differently than the rest — they clustered hard into six months, spiked around long weekends and backyard events, and needed a level of seasonal scheduling that a general pest line wasn't built around.

So Joe Barrett split it out. Mosquito Man Plus runs on the same research-led standards, the same Oshawa-based crew, and the same warranty-minded follow-through as Bugman Plus — just aimed at a single target, for the months it actually matters.

Meet the Founder

Joe Barrett, Oshawa, Ontario

Joe Barrett started Bugman Plus after seeing pest problems grow across the Canadian environment, building a company around careful inspection, honest pricing, and treatment that actually holds. Mosquito Man Plus is the next chapter of that same approach — narrowed to the pest that generates the most calls every summer, with its own schedule, its own products, and its own team focus.

Based in Oshawa, Joe and the crew work Durham Region and the GTA directly, which means the person quoting your job has usually stood in a yard like yours before.
