Service Guide

One brand, one pest, seven ways to handle it.

Every service below is built specifically around mosquito biology and Durham Region's spring–fall season — not adapted from a general pest control checklist.

01
Outdoor serviceSpring to fall

Residential Mosquito Barrier Treatment

Perimeter treatment along vegetation, shade lines, fences, and resting sites so your yard stays usable through peak mosquito season. This is the core visit most properties start with.
02
Recurring programSpring–fall visits

Seasonal Mosquito Programs

A scheduled run of barrier treatments spaced to hatch cycles and rainfall, so coverage holds all season instead of fading a few weeks after a single spray.
03
Event service24–48 hrs before

One-Time Event Spraying

Weddings, backyard parties, graduations, and other one-off gatherings treated ahead of time so guests can sit outside without fighting bugs all night.
04
Source controlSpring to fall

Standing Water & Breeding Site Treatment

Locate the water your property is actually breeding mosquitoes in — clogged gutters, low spots, containers, unused pools — and treat it directly at the source.
05
Larval stage controlSpring to fall

Larvicide Treatment

Target mosquitoes before they can fly, in water features and low spots that can't reasonably be drained or removed outright.
06
Add-on serviceSpring to fall

Tick Control Add-On

Bundle tick treatment into a scheduled mosquito visit for lawn edges, brush, and the shaded paths ticks favor. Not sold on its own — paired with a mosquito visit.
07
Commercial serviceSpring to fall

Commercial & Patio Mosquito Control

Restaurants, breweries, campgrounds, and patio businesses treated on a schedule that protects the customers actually sitting outside.

Not Sure Which Service?

Tell us what's happening and we'll match the treatment.

Most properties start with a barrier treatment or a seasonal program, then add tick control or a standing-water visit if the walk-through calls for it. Call, email, or send the quote form and we'll point you to the right one.
