Our Approach

Three steps, one focus: give you the yard back.

Mosquito Man Plus pairs a careful property walk with the right treatment, timing, and follow-through for your specific yard.

01

Inspect

Walk the property for standing water, shade, tall grass, and the resting sites where mosquitoes gather between feedings.

02

Treat & Protect

Apply a barrier treatment sized to the yard, with larvicide at any breeding sites that can't be removed outright.

03

Verify

Confirm the program is holding, share prevention steps for the areas we don't treat, and schedule the next visit before mosquitoes catch up.
